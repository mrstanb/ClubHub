User Schema [Authentication & Authorization] - email, password, first name, last name, gender, age, visited clubs (as array or smth similar), wanna go list (as array or something similar)

Club Schema - location (lng, lat), genre of music (as array or smth similar), events (as array or smth similar), working hours

Club Owner Schema [Authentication & Authorization] - email, password, clubs (as array or smth similar)

Events - date time, information, entry price
